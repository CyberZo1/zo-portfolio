Beauty and functionality in web Design

  • by Zo
  • May 23, 2024
  • Comments

Beauty and functionality are both important aspects of web design, and a successful website should balance the two. Aesthetics, or visual appeal, help users connect emotionally with a product, while functionality focuses on how well a product or system performs its intended purpose. In web design, aesthetics can help keep users engaged, while functionality refers to a website's features and abilities.

Accessibility and Usability in web design

  • by admin
  • May 23, 2024
  • Comments

Ensures that people with disabilities can perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with websites and tools. This includes the technical side of a website, such as ensuring that assistive technology can access the code. The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are internationally recognized standards that should be considered when developing web content. The four main guiding principles of WCAG are perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust, which is known as the acronym POUR. Focuses on the quality of a person's experience when interacting with a website, such as how easy the user interface is to use and how functional the design is. Usability includes user experience design.
